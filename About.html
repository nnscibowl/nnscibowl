About Science Bowl

Science bowl is a fast-paced buzzing game, where competing teams answer questions regarding subjects that range from Earth and Space, Biology, Physics, Math, and Energy. In other words, Science Bowl is perfect for anyone interested in science! While it is competitive, it's also about fun, sportsmanship, and ultimately, camaraderie.

At North

We're not just a club. We're a team. With a vibrant, supportive, and quirky culture, naperville north science bowl is so much more. With successful alumni, and dedicated upper-classmen, we provide unmatched and unconditional support for all our members, accelerating each to new heights
